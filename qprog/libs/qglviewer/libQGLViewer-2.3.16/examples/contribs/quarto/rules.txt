	Rules of the game of Quarto

The game:
 The board is composed of a 4x4 grid where 16 different pieces can be
 put. A piece is defined by 4 characteristics : small or tall, black or
 white, circle or square shape and with or without top bump.

Game play:
 A player selects a piece (right window) and gives it to the other player,
 who has to place it on the grid (left window). This player then selects a
 new piece that he gives to the first player, and so on.

End of the game:
 The first player who aligns (horizontally, vertically or in diagonal)
 four pieces with a common characteristic wins the game.
